The Gaston College SPARC3 program enhances the college experience of students studying Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM). Students in this program experience STEM classes that are interactive and hands-on. The program also provides scholarships to talented students with financial need (SPARC3 Scholars), personalized support services for students (i.e. advising, mentoring, tutoring, internships, and research experiences), and professional development for local educators.
